Security tips
Tips to prevent scams and fraud:
  • Never provide personal account info (like your SMS code, login or card number) by email, over the phone or text - we’ll only ever ask for things that will help identify you securely, like your verbal password or contact details
  • Never log in to online banking through an email link or third party website
  • We recommend against saving your password in your browser - find out how to prevent it
